Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for website development varies depending on complexity. A basic website might take 3 -4 days, while more complex projects with custom functionality could take 7-10 days. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a specific timeframe for your project.

What makes your AI chatbots different?

Our AI chatbots are built with advanced natural language processing capabilities that allow them to understand context and intent. They're customized to your business needs, integrated with your existing systems, and continuously learn from interactions to improve over time.

Do you offer website maintenance?

Yes, we offer various maintenance packages to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. Our maintenance services include regular updates, security monitoring, backup solutions, and technical support.

How do you measure the success of digital marketing campaigns?

We track key performance indicators (KPIs) specific to your business goals. These may include website traffic, conversion rates, lead generation, cost per acquisition, return on ad spend, and social media engagement. We provide regular reporting and analysis to demonstrate the ROI of our marketing efforts.
